Welcome to Bethany's Pie Shop

Our history

For many many years, bethany has been baking the most delicious pies at her home

In 2013, we opened our first store in brussels (Belgium) in a cosy little street near the Grand Market. People from all over world fell in love with the sort cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies... The store became a success in no time

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and Bethany's Pie Shop moved into a large building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it in pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking results of this: our very own online store, making it possible to order Bethany's delicious it!
